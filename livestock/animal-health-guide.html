Fundamentals of Animal Health

Maintaining good animal health is crucial for successful livestock farming. It involves preventive care, regular monitoring, and prompt treatment of health issues when they arise.

Key Health Indicators

  • Physical appearance
  • Behavior patterns
  • Feed consumption
  • Water intake
  • Body temperature

Daily Health Checks

  • Monitor eating habits
  • Check for injuries
  • Observe movement
  • Note unusual behavior
