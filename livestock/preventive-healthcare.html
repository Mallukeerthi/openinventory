Preventive Healthcare for Livestock

Essential practices for maintaining healthy animals

Last Updated: Mar 14, 2024 | 15 min read

Regular Health Monitoring

Daily Health Checks

  • Check feed and water consumption
  • Observe behavior and movement
  • Monitor body condition
  • Look for signs of illness or distress
  • Check for injuries or wounds

Vaccination Programs

Regular vaccinations are crucial for preventing common diseases in livestock.

Core Vaccinations

  • Follow age-appropriate vaccination schedules
  • Maintain vaccination records
  • Store vaccines properly
  • Use appropriate administration techniques

Biosecurity Measures

Critical Practices

  • Quarantine new animals
  • Control visitor access
  • Clean and disinfect facilities
  • Proper disposal of dead animals

Parasite Control

Regular Deworming

  • Schedule regular deworming treatments
  • Rotate pastures when possible
  • Monitor parasite loads
  • Keep records of treatments

Nutrition Management

Dietary Requirements

  • Provide balanced feed rations
  • Ensure clean water access
  • Monitor feed quality
  • Adjust diets seasonally

Record Keeping

Essential Records

  • Health treatments and outcomes
  • Vaccination dates
  • Medical expenses
  • Production data
